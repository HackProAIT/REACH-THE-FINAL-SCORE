GAME RULES:


- The game has 2 players, playing in rounds

- In each turn, a player rolls two dice as many times as he whishes. Each result get added to his ROUND score.

- BUT, if the player rolls a 1 on any dice or 6 on both dice, all his ROUND score gets lost. After that, it's the next player's turn.

- The player can choose to 'Hold', which means that his ROUND score gets added to his GLBAL score. After that, it's the next player's turn.

- The first player to reach value put on Final Score or by default 100 points on GLOBAL score wins the game.
